Data Preparation

Heat Index : What Temperature feels like to the human body when relative humidity is combined with the air Temperature.

Values:

80F - 90F : Caution, 90F - 103F : Extreme Caution, 103F - 124F : Danger, 125F or above : Extreme Danger.
