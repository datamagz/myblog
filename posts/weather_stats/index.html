Assumption & Hypothesis

Dependent Variables:

  • ‘Maximum Temperature’,
  • ‘Minimum Temperature’,
  • ‘Temperature’,
  • ‘Precipitation’,
  • ‘Relative Humidity’,
  • ‘Cloud Cover’,
  • ‘Wind Direction’,
  • ‘Wind Speed’,
  • ‘Visibility’

Independent variable:

  • ‘County’

Normality: We have 36162 observations, Central Limit Theorem (CLT) states that, as the sample size tends to infinity the distribution of sample means approaches the normal distribution i.e. a bell shaped curve.

Homogenity: Heteroskedastic

Hypothesis:

  • H0 = County geographical location give a effect to the weather variabels condition.
  • H1 = County geographical location do not give a significant effect to the weather variabels condition.
